Welcome to Ad Astra!


Who Are We?

We are a group of five individuals who utilizes FITS image headers to build a searchable data structure and simplify access to all images meeting specific criteria. Additionally, our software is able to identify potential asteroids or other dynamic objects. These are challenges provided by the CSA towards the NASA Space Apps Challenges Hackathon.


Our Goal

To be able to provide users, especially scientists, with a strong, reliable database to search for celestial bodies.


Next Steps

We would like to form a separate database for scientists and researchers, and add more functionalities for them to conduct more research. We would like to update our software for it be able to sync the FIT images uploaded by CSA directly onto the datebase.
